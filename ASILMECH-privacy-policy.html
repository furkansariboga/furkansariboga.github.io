Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains our approach regarding the collection and use of data while you use the ASILMECH mobile application.

ASILMECH does not integrate with any third-party service providers, analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Flurry), or advertising networks. Therefore, no data is shared with third parties.
