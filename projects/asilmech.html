🔧 What is ASILMECH?

ASILMECH is a mobile application designed to help engineering students quickly and effectively perform engineering calculations right from their phone.

Whether you're studying for an exam, working on a homework assignment, or just need a quick calculation on the go — ASILMECH has you covered!

It's educational in nature: the app is meant to help you learn and understand engineering concepts, not to replace proper design calculations.

⚡ Features

ASILMECH includes calculators and tools across multiple engineering disciplines:

▶ Strength of Materials

✔ Beam Bending - Simple supported beam deflection and stress
✔ Beam Deflection - Euler-Bernoulli deflection for various loading
✔ Mohr's Circle - Principal stresses (σ1, σ2) and max shear
✔ Torsion Calculation - Shear stress and angle of twist in shafts
✔ Axial Stress & Deformation - Normal stress and elongation
✔ Shear Stress & Strain - Average shear stress and angular deformation
✔ Engineering Strain - Axial, Shear, Thermal and Bending Strain

▶ Fluids, Thermodynamics & More

✔ Fluid mechanics calculators
✔ Thermodynamics tools
✔ Additional engineering utilities

▶ Electronics Tools

✔ Resistor Color Code Solver - Enter the band colors and get the full specs of any resistor (resistance, tolerance, etc.)

▶ General

✔ Search function - Quickly find any calculator (e.g. "Bearing", "Ohm"...)
✔ Favorites - Star your most-used tools for quick access
✔ Category tabs - Browse by Strength, Fluids, Thermo, and more
